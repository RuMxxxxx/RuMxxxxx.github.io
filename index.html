Golf Venues

Guangzhou Fengshen Golf Club – Dye Course

  • 18-HOLE PRIVATE MOUNTAIN COURSE
  • PETE DYE FAMILY DESIGN
  • HAWAII PLATINUM GRASS GREENS
  • 8500 SQ.M LUXURY CLUBHOUSE

Course Overview

Guangzhou Fengshen Golf Club is a world-class private golf resort located in the heart of Guangzhou's scenic mountainous area, designed by the legendary Pete Dye family, one of the most prestigious golf course designers in the world. The Dye Course, also known as the Mountain Course, opened in 2009 and features 18 championship holes with a par of 72 and a total length of 6,680 yards, perfectly blending natural mountain landscapes with professional golf course design.

Design Philosophy

The design philosophy of the Dye Course focuses on integrating natural terrain with strategic gameplay, utilizing the undulating mountain landscapes to create a soft yet elegant playing experience. The greens are planted with premium Hawaii Platinum Grass, ensuring a smooth and consistent putting surface, while the fairways feature high-quality Hawaii Grass that maintains its lush appearance year-round.

Club Facilities

The clubhouse is an 8,500-square-meter New Asian-style architectural masterpiece designed by the internationally acclaimed KTGY firm, featuring a unique saddle-shaped roof and expansive glass curtain walls that fill the space with natural light. The clubhouse offers 270-degree panoramic views of the golf course from every functional area.

Guangzhou Nansha Golf Club – Lake Course

  • LAKESIDE ECOLOGICAL COURSE
  • 9-HOLE LIGHTED NIGHT GOLF
  • SEASIDE LOCATION IN NANSHA
  • LUXURY VILLA ACCOMMODATION

Course Overview

Guangzhou Nansha Golf Club is a premier golf resort nestled in the ecological green belt of Nansha District, Guangzhou, master-planned by the visionary patriotic entrepreneur Sir Henry Fok. The club was designed by the renowned golf architect John Jacobs and officially opened in 1997.

Design Philosophy

The design philosophy of the Lake Course prioritizes harmony with nature, following the contours of the land to create undulating fairways and diverse playing challenges. Unlike competitive tournament courses, the bunkers are moderately designed, focusing on leisure and enjoyment.

Club Facilities

The club offers comprehensive luxury facilities, including a 7,000-square-meter clubhouse, water and land driving ranges, an indoor tennis hall, hotel rooms, and exclusive villa accommodation. The clubhouse is centrally located near the 9th and 18th greens.

Shenzhen Noble Merchant Golf Club

  • DOWNTOWN SHENZHEN LOCATION
  • 18-HOLE PROFESSIONAL NIGHT COURSE
  • CASTLE-STYLE CLUBHOUSE
  • STATE BANQUET CUISINE SERVICE

Course Overview

Shenzhen Noble Merchant Golf Club is a unique urban golf oasis located on Shennan Avenue, the central business district of Shenzhen, offering golfers a convenient and luxurious playing experience without leaving the city.

Night Golf Highlights

The night golf experience at Noble Merchant is the club's flagship feature, designed with optimal lighting from the initial planning stages. The lighting system is evenly distributed, eliminating shadows and providing clear visibility of fairways.

Club Facilities

The club's castle-style clubhouse, designed by the British PMJM firm, exudes elegance and luxury, especially when illuminated at night. The facility includes deluxe suites, fine Chinese and Western restaurants, a cozy bar, and a fully equipped fitness center.
